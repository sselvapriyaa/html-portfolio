Malala Yousafzai

“One child, one teacher, one book, one pen can change the world. Education is the only solution. Education first.” Malala is the youngest person ever to win the Nobel Peace Prize for her fight for the right of every child to receive an education.

Malala Yousafzai became an international symbol of the fight for girls’ education after she was shot in 2012 for opposing Taliban restrictions on female education in her home country of Pakistan.
